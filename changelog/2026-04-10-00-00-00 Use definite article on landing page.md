# Use definite article on landing page

## What changed

Updated three instances on the landing page (`app/page.tsx`) where Primordia referred to itself with the indefinite article "a/an" — changed to the definite article "the":

- Sub-headline: `A web application that rewrites itself on demand.` → `The web application that rewrites itself on demand.`
- Features section: `It's an app that builds itself.` → `It's the app that builds itself.`
- Footer tagline: `Primordia — a self-modifying web application` → `Primordia — the self-modifying web application`

## Why

Using "the" instead of "a/an" signals that Primordia is a specific, singular thing — not one of many generic web apps. It reinforces brand identity and makes the copy feel more confident and distinct.
